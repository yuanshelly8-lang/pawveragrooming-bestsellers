About Pawvera Grooming

Pawvera Grooming is a B2B pet grooming tool sourcing and private label project brand operated by Hangzhou Misishell Import & Export Co., Ltd.. We work with manufacturing partners rather than presenting Pawvera as the factory for every model.

Who Operates Pawvera Grooming?

Pawvera Grooming is operated by Hangzhou Misishell Import & Export Co., Ltd., a Hangzhou-based export and sourcing company. Our role is to help buyers compare pet clipper models, coordinate samples, discuss branding and packaging, and manage order communication with manufacturing partners.

How We Describe Our Supply Chain

We do not use one blanket factory, certification or test claim for every model. Factory information, production capability, certificates and battery documents are confirmed for the selected product and destination market before the order is finalized.
