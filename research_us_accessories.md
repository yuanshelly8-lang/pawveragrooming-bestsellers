**Summary for OEM Strategy:** 
- **Young's Inc.** and **Best Bling Bling** are the strongest candidates for high-volume wholesale distribution partnerships.
- **Bling Car Decor** and **Popfizzy** are excellent targets for private label or specialized OEM product development in the car and keychain niches respectively.
