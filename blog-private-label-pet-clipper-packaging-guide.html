Private Label Pet Clipper Packaging: What to Specify Before Production

Packaging is where many first-time private label buyers lose money. Underspecifying at the order stage leads to corrections, reprints, and delays. This guide tells you exactly what to confirm before production begins.

Why Packaging Matters More Than You Think

For pet clipper private label brands, the retail box is the first thing your end customer touches. It creates the initial impression that either confirms or undermines the premium positioning you are trying to build. On Amazon and bol.com, packaging is also scrutinised in unboxing videos — a poorly fitted insert or a dull box finish will appear in your negative reviews.

From a compliance standpoint, packaging in the EU must carry specific labelling: CE mark, battery disposal symbol, manufacturer name and address, and country of origin. Missing these can trigger a bol.com or Amazon compliance hold. See our full compliance documentation →

The 5 Packaging Elements to Specify Before Signing a Production Order

1. Retail Box Dimensions and Material

Box dimensions should be designed around the clipper plus all accessories — comb guides, cleaning brush, oil bottle, USB-C cable, and manual. Confirm the dimensions once you have approved the final accessory set. Standard pet clipper retail boxes range from 220mm × 120mm × 80mm (compact) to 280mm × 160mm × 100mm (full-kit). Material is usually 350gsm coated cardboard for the outer box.

2. Artwork and Colour Specification

Provide your artwork as a print-ready PDF or AI file with a minimum of 300 DPI. Specify Pantone colours for any brand-critical elements — do not rely on CMYK approximations for important brand colours. Include both the outer box design and the inner tray design in your artwork brief. Confirm bleed and safety margin requirements with your supplier's print team before finalising the file.

3. EU Compliance Labelling

Every pet clipper retail box sold in the EU must carry:

  • CE marking (must correspond to your actual CE certificate model)
  • WEEE directive symbol (crossed-out wheelie bin) for electrical products
  • Battery disposal symbol (crossed-out wheelie bin with battery icon)
  • Manufacturer name and EU responsible person address
  • Country of origin: Made in China
  • Model number and relevant technical specifications (voltage, input/output)

For Amazon EU listings, the barcode (EAN/GTIN) must also appear on the outside of the retail box. For Amazon FBA, include the FNSKU label as well. Read our CE compliance guide for details →

4. Insert Tray Specification

The inner insert tray holds the clipper and accessories in place during transit and display. Specify the material (EVA foam, thermoformed plastic, or pulp moulded paper) based on your price point and sustainability positioning. For premium price tiers, a thermoformed plastic tray with individual cutouts for each accessory is the expected standard. For mid-tier products, a two-piece cardboard insert with die-cut windows is acceptable and significantly cheaper.

5. Manual Language and Content

Your product manual must be in the language of your primary market. For EU multi-country distribution, most buyers include at minimum: English, German, French, Dutch, and Spanish. Japanese and Korean are required for those markets respectively. Pawvera can prepare a standard technical manual template in your required languages — confirm the language list before production begins.
