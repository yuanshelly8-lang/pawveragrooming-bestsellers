Pet Clipper Importer Checklist: Before You Place a Bulk Order

A practical bulk-order checklist covering model confirmation, packaging, labels, quality checks, shipping and sales materials.

A practical bulk-order checklist covering model confirmation, packaging, labels, quality checks, shipping and sales materials.

Confirm the final model code and configuration

Use the exact model code in the quotation, proforma invoice, sample approval and carton marks. Confirm color, blade, battery, accessories and plug type.

Document packaging and branding

Keep approved logo files, box artwork, manual text, barcode labels and carton marks together. Small packaging changes can affect price and production time.

Define quality inspection points

Common checks include appearance, switch operation, charging, runtime sampling, blade movement, accessory count, packaging condition and carton labeling.

Prepare product content before shipment

Request clean product images, specifications and package information early so listings and sales pages can be prepared while production is underway.

Plan shipping details

Confirm carton quantity, gross weight, dimensions, shipping marks and delivery terms. The best shipping method depends on order size, urgency and destination.

Create a reorder record

Keep the approved sample, specification sheet and packaging files as the standard for repeat orders. This reduces misunderstandings when the product line grows.
