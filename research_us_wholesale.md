## 4. Key Trade Shows for Rhinestone Sourcing

- **ASD Market Week (Las Vegas):** The largest B2B general merchandise trade show in the US, featuring a massive "Gift & Jewelry" section with numerous rhinestone importers and wholesalers.
- **AmericasMart Atlanta:** A major hub for gift and apparel accessories, specifically during the Atlanta Market events.
- **Dallas Market Center:** Hosts the "Total Home & Gift Market" where brands like Savvy Bling showcase crystal-embellished products to boutique buyers.
