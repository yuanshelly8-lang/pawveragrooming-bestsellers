Factory Partner Verification

Pawvera works with manufacturing partners and verifies factory information by project. We do not claim that one factory statement applies to every model.

What “factory partner” means

Pawvera Grooming is the buyer-facing sourcing and project coordination brand. Manufacturing is completed by selected production partners. The relevant factory, production line and evidence should be confirmed for the exact model being quoted.

ConfirmedBuyer-facing company

Hangzhou Misishell Import & Export Co., Ltd. operates Pawvera Grooming and coordinates sourcing communication.

Project-specificManufacturing partner

The factory is identified for the selected model and order configuration.

On requestProduction evidence

Available factory photos, videos, audit information or production records are checked before sharing.

No blanket claimCapacity statements

Capacity, employee count and years in business are not published without supporting evidence.

Factory Verification Checklist

  • Legal company name and business license
  • Model production responsibility
  • Assembly and testing process
  • Quality-control records
  • Battery and component source
  • Packaging and labeling capability

What Buyers Should Request

  • Factory or supplier identity for the quoted model
  • Current product and packaging photos
  • Applicable test reports
  • Sample approval record
  • Pre-shipment inspection checklist
  • Written lead time and defect-handling terms

Need Model-Specific Factory Evidence?

Send the model, quantity and destination market so the relevant partner information can be checked.
