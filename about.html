Our Story

The Dog Who Started Everything

Pawvera didn't start in a boardroom. It started with a 14-year-old dog named Mochi, a terrified grooming session, and a question that wouldn't go away.

In 2018, Shelly took her 14-year-old Bichon Frise, Mochi, to a professional grooming salon in Hangzhou. The clipper the groomer used was loud. Mochi shook through the entire session. She refused to eat for the rest of the day.

Shelly went home and searched for a quieter alternative. What she found was a fragmented, confusing market — hundreds of products making noise claims, almost none of them backed by actual measurements. The ones that were genuinely quiet were either unavailable in China or priced for professional salons, not for someone buying one unit at a time.

I started asking around — suppliers, factory contacts, people in the pet product trade. Everyone said the same thing: low-noise clippers exist, they're technically straightforward, but nobody is selling them properly to buyers who actually care. — Shelly, Founder, Pawvera Grooming

A Problem Worth Solving

Shelly's background was in international trade — she had spent years connecting Chinese manufacturers with overseas buyers across several product categories. She understood exactly what it took to make a supply chain work from both sides: what buyers in the Netherlands and Germany were looking for, what Chinese factories were capable of, and where the gap was.

The gap in pet clippers was clear. Overseas distributors and Amazon sellers needed products that were genuinely CE certified, genuinely quiet, and genuinely available with DDP shipping and real compliance documents — not a self-signed declaration and a vague promise about transit times.

Pawvera Grooming was built to close that gap.

What We Built

We partnered with a CE-certified manufacturing facility in China's Ningbo-Hangzhou manufacturing corridor — one with a real QC lab, an actual noise measurement process, and production capacity that could support a growing international customer base.

We set non-negotiable standards from the start: every model we carry must be third-party CE certified. Every battery shipment must have UN38.3 and MSDS documentation. Every DDP quote must be a real, all-in number — not a landing price that surprises you with additional charges.

Our first overseas wholesale order was from a pet salon owner in Amsterdam. She emailed three times asking whether the noise measurement was real or marketing. We sent her a sample and told her to measure it herself. She ordered 80 units the next week. — Shelly

Why We Still Do It

Mochi passed away in 2022, at 18 years old. She groomed quietly for the last four years of her life. Every time a customer tells us their anxious cat finally sat still through a grooming session, or that their dog no longer hides when they take out the clipper, we remember why this matters.

We're not in the business of making the cheapest clipper or the most impressive spec sheet. We're in the business of making the right product — one that performs as described, ships with real documentation, and arrives without surprises. That's what Mochi deserved. It's what your customers deserve too.
