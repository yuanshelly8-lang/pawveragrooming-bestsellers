## Top Amazon Brands (Likely Importers/Private Label)
These brands dominate the Amazon search results with high volume and BSR (Best Sellers Rank). They are often potential targets for OEM supply chain improvements.

*   **Daofaith:** Top seller of "Rhinestone Tumbler Cups" on Amazon. High review counts (1000+ per listing).
*   **Rtteri:** Massive variety of bling water bottles and insulated tumblers.
*   **Tessco:** Sells various bling and glitter tumblers in bulk sets.
*   **XMYAINA:** Specializes in 20oz/30oz/40oz bling tumblers with handles.

*   **Novelty Inc:** (Mentioned above) - Primary for retail/convenience channels.

**Note:** Many boutique "bling" companies are small businesses (SOLO entrepreneurs) who hand-place thousands of stones. The larger entities (TRW, Novelty Inc, Be Createful) are the primary movers of volume in the US market.
