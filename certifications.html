Certification FAQ

Are your pet clippers CE certified
CE marking is available for models sold into EU markets. The specific Declaration of Conformity and supporting test reports are confirmed per model and destination country. We do not apply one CE certificate across all models — each certification is model-specific. Contact us with your target model to receive the correct documents.
Can I get battery shipping documents for air freight
Yes. Our SM 18500-2000 3.7V lithium battery is UN38.3 certified, which covers both air and sea freight requirements. MSDS documentation is also available. These documents are provided after the model and shipping route are confirmed.
What certifications are required to sell pet clippers on bol.com Netherlands
For bol.com (Netherlands), pet clippers require CE marking under the Low Voltage Directive (LVD) and EMC Directive. RoHS compliance is also mandatory. Bol.com may request a Declaration of Conformity and, in some cases, third-party test reports from accredited laboratories such as SGS, TÜV, or Intertek. We can provide these documents for the confirmed model.
Do you provide third-party lab test reports
Test reports from third-party accredited laboratories are available for specific models. The availability depends on the target market and product model. Please confirm your model and destination country so we can check which reports are available or need to be arranged.
How do I request certification documents
Contact us via WhatsApp or email with: (1) the product model you are ordering, (2) your destination country or market, (3) your intended sales channel (bol.com, Amazon, retail store, etc.), and (4) any specific certification standard required. We will confirm which documents are available and provide them before order confirmation.
