The Pawvera Production Process

Every wholesale order goes through a controlled 5-stage process designed to protect your brand and your customers.

📋

1. Order Confirmation

Model selection, OEM requirements, packaging specs, and certification documents confirmed before production begins.

🏭

2. Production

Assembly line production with dedicated workstations for your model batch. Motor testing, blade fitting, and LCD calibration at each station.

🔬

3. QC Inspection

5-point QC check: appearance, function, accessories, branding, and packaging. Pre-shipment sample photos sent to buyer for approval.

📦

4. Packaging

Custom color retail box, branded inner tray, accessory set confirmed, FNSKU / barcode labels applied per destination market.

🚢

5. DDP Shipping

Door-to-door delivery to Netherlands, Germany, UK, US and Australia. Sea or air freight. Full shipping documents provided.

What We Can Deliver for Your Brand

⚙️

OEM / Private Label

Full branding customization available for wholesale buyers launching their own pet grooming line.

  • Laser-engraved logo on clipper body
  • Full-color custom retail packaging
  • Branded manual in your language
  • Custom accessory set (comb guides, oils)

Quality Control

In-house QC lab with precision testing equipment and a dedicated pre-shipment inspection process.

  • Noise level measurement (target: <55dB)
  • Battery runtime test (full charge cycle)
  • Motor RPM verification
  • Blade sharpness and fit check
  • Drop test and vibration test per IEC standard
🚢

Export & Logistics

Experienced international logistics team handling DDP shipments to major markets worldwide.

  • DDP door-to-door to EU, US, UK, AU
  • Sea freight (28–35 days to Rotterdam)
  • Air freight (7–10 days express)
  • Amazon FBA-ready labeling and prep
  • UN38.3 + MSDS battery shipping docs
