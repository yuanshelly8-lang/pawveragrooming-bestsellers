Buyer Project Workflows

These are transparent workflow examples, not named customer success claims. They show how a wholesale pet clipper project can move from model selection to shipment.

Why we use workflows instead of invented case studies

Until a customer authorizes publication, Pawvera does not publish names, order values, sales results or testimonials as facts. These workflow examples explain the process buyers can expect.

Illustrative workflow

Distributor Model Selection

Buyer compares C28, C59 and D88 by runtime, charging method, blade and target price level, then requests samples before choosing a range.

  1. Share market and target price position
  2. Compare models and specifications
  3. Request samples
  4. Confirm packaging and documentation
  5. Approve order terms
Illustrative workflow

Private Label E-commerce Launch

Buyer selects one model, confirms logo placement, retail box, manual language and barcode, then approves a branded sample.

  1. Choose model and accessory set
  2. Submit logo and packaging files
  3. Confirm sample artwork
  4. Approve sample and QC checklist
  5. Start bulk production
Illustrative workflow

Salon Supply Product Line

Buyer prioritizes professional appearance, blade configuration, runtime and spare-part planning for salon channels.

  1. Define salon buyer requirements
  2. Review A83, P45 and C59
  3. Confirm spare parts and warranty terms
  4. Test sample performance
  5. Finalize shipping documents
