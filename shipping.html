Shipping FAQ

What does DDP mean for my import?
DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) means Pawvera arranges and pays for all freight costs, export procedures, international transport, destination customs clearance, import duties, and local delivery to your warehouse address. You receive the goods without interacting with any customs authority or logistics provider. The DDP price you receive is the total landed cost.
Can you ship lithium battery pet clippers by air freight?
Yes. Our SM 18500-2000 3.7V lithium batteries are UN38.3 certified, which is the required certification for air transport of lithium cells. We also provide MSDS documentation. Both documents are included in every air freight shipment documentation package as standard.
Do you ship to Amazon FBA warehouses?
Yes. We ship directly to Amazon FBA fulfillment centres in the EU, UK, and US. Products are prepared with FNSKU labels, suffocation warning stickers, and poly-bag sealing as required by Amazon receiving standards. Confirm your FBA centre address and any prep requirements at order confirmation stage.
How is the DDP price calculated?
DDP pricing is based on: product unit cost + OEM costs (if applicable) + sea or air freight + insurance + destination import duty + VAT (where applicable) + local delivery. We quote all-in DDP prices per unit — no surprises on arrival. Prices vary by destination country and freight method. Request a quote with your destination and order quantity.
What is the minimum order for DDP shipping?
For stocked models, we accept B2B wholesale orders from 20 units with DDP service. For OEM/private label orders with custom packaging, please contact us to discuss minimum quantities and lead times based on your specific requirements.
