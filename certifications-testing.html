Certifications, Test Reports & Battery Documents

Certification and logistics documents vary by model, battery, destination market and sales channel. This page explains what must be verified instead of making blanket claims.

Evidence policy

Pawvera Grooming only presents a certificate or test report as applicable after matching the report holder, product model, technical specification and target market. A document available for one product does not automatically apply to the full range.

Before Accepting a Document

  • Match the applicant or manufacturer name
  • Match the exact product model or technical family
  • Check report date and laboratory
  • Check standards and destination market
  • Confirm battery information for shipping
  • Keep the final document with the order record

Statements We Avoid Without Proof

  • “All models are CE certified”
  • “UL listed” without listing evidence
  • “FDA approved” for a pet grooming clipper
  • “Certified factory” without audit scope
  • “100% defect free”
  • “Worldwide compliant”
