What Your Sample Lets You Verify

🔇

Actual Noise Level

Use a free dB meter app to measure operating noise yourself. Our low-noise models measure below 55dB at 1 meter under real-world conditions — not lab conditions.

🔋

Real Battery Runtime

Run the clipper continuously from full charge to empty and measure the runtime yourself. We stand behind the stated battery specifications.

✂️

Blade Sharpness

Test the blade on real pet fur — thick, thin, curly or straight. The ceramic-titanium blade combination should glide without pulling or snagging.

🏗️

Build Quality

Check the seam alignment, button feel, charging port fit, and weight balance. What you see in the sample is what your end customers will receive.

🎨

OEM Branding Preview

For OEM orders, your sample can include a prototype logo placement — so you can approve the engraving position and finish before bulk production begins.

📋

Compliance Documents

Your sample shipment arrives with the relevant CE declaration, UN38.3 battery certificate, and MSDS — so you can verify compliance before committing.
