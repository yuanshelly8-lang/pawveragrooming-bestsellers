OEM Private Label Pet Clippers: A Buyer’s Guide

Understand logo customization, packaging, samples, label pet grooming clipper projects.

Understand logo customization, packaging, samples, label pet grooming clipper projects.

What private label customization usually includes

A private label project can include the logo on the clipper body, a custom color box, user manual, insert card, barcode labels and shipping carton marks. The available options depend on the model and order quantity.

Select a model before designing packaging

Choose the product and accessory configuration first. Packaging dimensions, inserts and product photography all depend on the final model and included accessories.

Confirm logo method and artwork

Provide vector artwork when possible and confirm the logo position, size and color. Request a visual proof or branded sample before mass production.

Approve a complete sample

The approval sample should reflect the machine, accessories, logo and packaging as closely as possible. Create a written approval record so the production standard is clear.

Prepare market-specific information

Confirm plug type, voltage, manual language, barcode requirements, warning labels and any certification documents needed for the destination market.

Build a repeat-order product line

A strong private label program should include a starter model, a premium model and compatible accessories. This gives distributors and online sellers room to create bundles and price tiers.
