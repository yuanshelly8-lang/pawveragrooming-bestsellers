6 Reasons Buyers Stick With Pawvera

01

You Talk to the Source

When you contact Pawvera, you're communicating directly with the people managing the manufacturing relationship — not a trading company representative who has never visited the factory. Every quote, every spec, every compliance question goes straight to someone who can verify and confirm.

02

CE Documents That Pass Audits

Our CE Certificate of Conformity (LCS190729008CE) was issued by an accredited third-party laboratory — Guangzhou LCS Compliance Testing Laboratory Ltd. — not self-signed. This is what bol.com, Amazon EU, and retail compliance audits actually require. Many buyers discover this distinction only after their shipment is held.

03

DDP Takes the Complexity Out

Importing electronics from China means dealing with HS codes, import duties, lithium battery airfreight regulations, and customs clearance paperwork. Under DDP, we handle all of it. Your goods arrive at your warehouse door. You receive an invoice. That's the entire process on your side.

04

Low MOQ, Real Scale When You're Ready

We accept wholesale orders from 20 units for stocked models — low enough for a market test or a first bol.com listing. When your order proves itself and you're ready to scale to 500, 2,000, or 10,000 units, the pricing, quality, and process stay consistent.

05

Production You Can Actually See

For bulk orders, we send you production photos every two days during manufacturing. You see the products being made, the packaging being assembled, the QC inspection. No surprises on arrival. If something needs adjusting, you see it while there's still time to adjust.

06

Tested Before Shipped

Every batch goes through a 5-point QC inspection before leaving the warehouse: appearance, function (battery runtime, motor speed, noise), accessories, branding verification, and packaging integrity. The pre-shipment sample approval process means you sign off before the container is sealed.
