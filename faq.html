Wholesale Pet Clipper FAQ

Procurement answers for distributors, importers, e-commerce sellers and private label brands. Commercial terms are confirmed for each model and project.

Quick procurement answer

Pawvera Grooming supports sample evaluation, OEM logo and packaging coordination, model comparison and project-specific quality documentation. Send your model, quantity, market and customization requirements for an accurate quote.

Can I request a sample?

Yes. Sample availability, sample cost and preparation time are confirmed for the selected model, branding request and destination country before payment.

Can I add my own logo?

Yes. Machine-body logo, color box, manual, barcode labels and carton marks can be discussed before sample approval.

How long does sample preparation take?

Sample preparation time is quoted after the model, plug type, accessory set, logo and packaging requirements are confirmed.

What is the bulk production lead time?

Bulk lead time depends on quantity, customization, component availability and packaging complexity. The confirmed timing should be written into the quotation or proforma invoice.

Which certificates or test reports are available?

Documents are model- and market-specific. Available reports are checked for the selected product before order confirmation; Pawvera does not apply one certificate claim to every model.

How is the product packed?

Standard export packing is available. Retail color boxes, manuals, labels, inserts and accessory combinations can be quoted separately for private label projects.

How do you control quality?

The project can include incoming material checks, function testing, charging and runtime checks, blade inspection, appearance inspection and pre-shipment sampling. The final checklist is confirmed by model.

Can you support battery shipping documents?

Battery-related shipping documents depend on the battery cell and route. MSDS, UN38.3 or other logistics documents are confirmed for the selected model and shipping plan.

What after-sales information should buyers confirm?

Before ordering, confirm the defect-handling method, evidence requirements, spare-parts plan, warranty scope and response timeline in writing.

Which buyers are these products suitable for?

The range is designed for distributors, importers, e-commerce sellers, pet grooming brands, pet shops and salon-supply buyers.

What information is needed for an accurate quote?

Please send the model, quantity, destination country, target market, plug type, logo file, packaging requirements, certification needs and preferred delivery time.
